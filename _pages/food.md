As mentioned, one of my favorite things to do in my free time is finding new places to eat. I am lucky to live in California! Below are a list of top five (ish) restaurants by cuisine. It was hard to choose only five, but if I had it my way, this list would go on forever. <br>

If you have any recommendations for places to try, please feel free to shoot me an email! :)

2. QQ Noodle (Cupertino)
